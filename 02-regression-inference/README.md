## Work completed (example)

Maintenance expense modeled with **Miles Driven** and **Age of Truck**:

| Predictor | Coefficient | p-value | Significant at α = 0.05? |
|-----------|-------------|---------|--------------------------|
| Intercept | 11.42 | 0.700 | — |
| Miles Driven | 0.071 | ≈ 6.9×10⁻⁸ | Yes |
| Age of Truck | 10.71 | 0.257 | No |

**Model:** ŷ = 11.42 + 0.071·(Miles Driven) + 10.71·(Age of Truck)  
**R²:** 0.951 · **n:** 23
